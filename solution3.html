What is this about

Find out how oil use affects the climate and environment through graphs, and find out how to reduce this use.

Why does investing help?

Community investment in rainforests helps protect biodiversity, supports sustainable livelihoods, reduces deforestation, and maintains the forest's role in carbon sequestration, benefiting both local communities and the global ecosystem.

How can we invest to the community?

Invest in rainforest communities by supporting sustainable agriculture, eco-tourism, non-timber forest products, and conservation education to create economic opportunities that preserve the rainforest.
