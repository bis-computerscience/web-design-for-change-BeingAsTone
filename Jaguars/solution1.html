Reduce Wood & Paper Use

Reducing the use of paper and wood can have a huge impact

and help jaguars survive.

What is this about

By looking at the usage of each type of paper, we realize the seriousness of the problem, how this usage affects jaguars, and how we can help.

How is the paper consumption?

Many regions are showing enormous paper consumption. And it still hasn't shown any significant changes to this day. Since most paper is produced from trees, many trees ultimately have to be cut down. Deforestation ultimately destroys the Amazon environment, causing many jaguars to lose their home.

How can we help reducing the use?

The use of wood and paper products ultimately causes deforestation. You can help effectively by reducing your paper and wood consumption by using both sides of paper, using cloth bags, napkins and towels, and avoiding disposable items.
