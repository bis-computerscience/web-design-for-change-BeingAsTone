What is this about

Find out how oil use affects the climate and environment through graphs, and find out how to reduce this use.

What is the impact of oil consumption?

Oil production significantly impacts climate, particularly global warming, causing significant environmental degradation. This devastation affects jaguars, making their survival difficult due to the destruction of their natural habitat.

How can we help reducing the use?

Climate change threatens global stability by burning oil, gas, and coal, destroying jaguar habitats. Preventing it involves reducing oil consumption, using fuel-efficient cars, avoiding sport utility vehicles, and using public transportation.
